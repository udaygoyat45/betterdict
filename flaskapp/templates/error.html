About us

This is a dictionary app but with a twist. Every time you look up a word, you store it in your short term memory to understand the text you are learning. There are two problems with this method. After googling the word's meaning, you sometimes forget the word's meaning after a while; furthermore, when you are speaking and writing you don't use that word you googled. There is a solution to this, this web application will remind you the word on a weekly basis and encourage you to recall the word and use it in daily writing. Simply put, this app will help you put the complicated words in your active vocabulary.
